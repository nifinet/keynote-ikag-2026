The hypothesis

Where did the small projects go?

Hypothesis, not a fact

The simplest projects (a basic logo, a 5-page site, a simple ad campaign) are increasingly absorbed by tools: Canva, ChatGPT, Wix, Midjourney.

We can't prove this from our data alone. But when the share of larger budgets rises while volume drops, the floor rising is the simplest explanation.

The projects that remain on Sortlist are the ones that can't be solved by a tool.

The central thesis

Which side are you on?

Don't panic.

Your service isn't dying. The label is changing.

"We need someone to build our online marketing strategy, manage our campaigns, and track ROI."

Two years ago, that brief was filed under "Digital Strategy".
Today, the exact same brief is filed under "Marketing".

Same client. Same deliverable. Same agency. Different checkbox.

If your category is "declining," check whether the work just moved to another label.
